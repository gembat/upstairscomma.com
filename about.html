About

Sara McSorley

I'm a designer and illustrator currently residing in Lexington, Kentucky. My art has been featured on the Bioware blog, ShirtPunch, and has sold in the Desert Bus For Hope live auction.

I have experience designing logos, creating brand identities, and illustrating book covers.

If you'd like to hire me for a freelance job, please send me an email. You can find more specifics about commissioning me on this page.

In my spare time I frequent conventions and pretend to be involved in a maple syrup conspiracy.
